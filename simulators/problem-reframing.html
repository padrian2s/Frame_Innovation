About This Simulator

A frame is a standpoint from which a problem situation is viewed — a pattern of relationships that shapes what we see, what we ignore, and what solutions become possible. The same complex problem, seen through different frames, yields entirely different strategies. Explore four real-world scenarios drawn from Dorst's work and see how reframing transforms the solution space.
