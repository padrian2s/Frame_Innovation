Frame Creation Process

Dorst's 9-step model for creating new frames in complex problem situations

About This Simulator

Frame creation is a structured approach to tackling open, complex problems that resist conventional solutions. Kees Dorst's 9-step model guides you from archaeological excavation of the problem through paradox identification, context mapping, and theme discovery toward the creation of entirely new frames that redefine the problem space. Work through each step sequentially, recording your insights along the way.
