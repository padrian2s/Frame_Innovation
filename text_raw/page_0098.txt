ESTABLISHING THE CORE PARADOX

The core paradox that the Sydney Opera House originally faced, under pressure
to take measures to prevent people from climbing up the sails, can be paraphrased as:
Because the Sydney Opera House is such a special place and an iconic building, it attracts protesters who seek attention.
Because the Sydney Opera House is such an iconic building, it cannot be
touched/altered (due to heritage listing).
