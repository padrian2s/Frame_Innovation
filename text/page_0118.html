Chapter 5: Principles of Frame Creation

Commentary

Summary

Understanding principles is more important than following steps mechanically. Principles allow adaptation to new contexts and development of personal style.

Key Patterns

Principles emphasize openness, depth, collaboration, and attraction. Quality depends on investigation and engagement more than creative brilliance. Thoroughness enables creativity.

Practical Applications

Practice the attract principle: design solutions people want to participate in. Ask: would stakeholders choose this voluntarily? If not, reconsider the frame.
