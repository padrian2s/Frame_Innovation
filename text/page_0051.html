Learning from the Pioneers

Commentary

Summary

Common design approaches among pioneers: resist premature problem definition, engage deeply with context, work across disciplines, create new frames rather than optimizing within existing ones.

Key Patterns

Pioneer organizations share a pattern: deep engagement with context, resistance to obvious framings, cross-disciplinary work, and solutions fundamentally different from what conventional approaches would generate.

Practical Applications

Study how pioneers engage: start with deep understanding, not solutions. Spend more time understanding what people need before proposing what you can offer. Look for the 'real' problem behind the stated one.
