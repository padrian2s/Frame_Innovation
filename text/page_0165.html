The Role of Experimentation

Commentary

Summary

Experimentation: willingness to try, learn, iterate. Different from 'plan then execute.' Experiments are learning opportunities that strengthen the final solution.

Key Patterns

Seeing, thinking, and doing differently are interrelated. All three must be addressed. The challenges reflect deep assumptions about rationality, identity, and practice.

Practical Applications

Address three challenges simultaneously: create opportunities for seeing through others' eyes, introduce broader notions of valid reasoning, and protect experimental spaces for new approaches.
