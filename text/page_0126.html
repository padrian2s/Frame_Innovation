The Attract Principle

Commentary

Summary

The attract principle: solutions so compelling that stakeholders want to participate. Instead of forcing compliance, create solutions people are drawn to. Chosen solutions are more sustainable than imposed ones.

Attract Principle: Solutions so compelling stakeholders want to participate voluntarily, rather than being forced through compliance.

Key Patterns

Principles emphasize openness, depth, collaboration, and attraction. Quality depends on investigation and engagement more than creative brilliance. Thoroughness enables creativity.

Practical Applications

Practice the attract principle: design solutions people want to participate in. Ask: would stakeholders choose this voluntarily? If not, reconsider the frame.
